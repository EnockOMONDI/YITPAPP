The Youth Impact Training Programme (YITP) is a 9-week virtual training programe designed to equip young people with knowledge, essential soft skills, and a transformative mindset to help them succeed in their careers or entrepreneurial journeys. Our mission is to bridge the gap between education and real-world opportunities by offering:

  • Training in modern skills, personal growth, and mindset change
  • Business mentorship for young entrepreneurs
  • Expert advice on youth challenges
  • Apprenticeship opportnities.

Elevate Your Future Today!

The Youth Impact Training Programme (YITP) is more than just a course. —it’s a launchpad for success! Plus, earn a recognized certification upon completion.

Our in-person and virtual training offers knowledge and green skills for the 21st century. This knowledge will help young people become partners in solving the unemployment crisis, becoming entrepreneurs, and securing financial stability and freedom.

Our short course, currently being offered virtually, runs for nine weeks (two months and one week), with after-training clustering for mentorship, networking, and business/career coaching follow-up for a year.
