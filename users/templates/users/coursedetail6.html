CV Writing

Unit Title: CV Writing

Objective: Equip participants with the knowledge and skills to create an effective CV and cover letter that enhances job application success.

Topic 1: Introduction to CVs

Objective: Understand what a CV is and why it is important.

  • Definition of a CV
  • The purpose and importance of a CV in job applications
Activity: Brainstorm key elements that make a CV stand out.

Topic 2: Types of CVs

Objective: Explore different CV formats and when to use them.

  • Chronological CV
  • Functional CV
  • Combination CV
Activity: Identify the best CV type based on individual career paths.

Topic 3: CV Templates

Objective: Understand the impact of different CV templates on presentation.

  • Traditional CV templates
  • Modern CV templates
  • Creative CV templates
Activity: Choose and customize a CV template that best represents you.

Topic 4: Cover Letters

Objective: Learn how to craft a compelling cover letter.

  • Definition, purpose, and importance of a cover letter
  • How to structure a strong cover letter
Activity: Draft a personalized cover letter based on a job posting.

Topic 5: Cover Letter Templates

Objective: Understand different cover letter formats.

  • Basic cover letter templates
  • Intermediate cover letter templates
  • Advanced cover letter templates
Activity: Adapt an existing cover letter template to match your experience.

Topic 6: Applying for Jobs with a CV and Cover Letter

Objective: Master the process of job applications.

  • How to tailor your CV and cover letter for different jobs
  • Effective job application strategies
  • Common mistakes to avoid
Activity: Submit a CV and cover letter for review and feedback.

Topic 7: Creating Your CV and Cover Letter

Objective: Hands-on training in CV and cover letter creation.

  • Step-by-step guide to writing a CV
  • Best practices for formatting and readability
Activity: Build a personalized CV and cover letter.

Topic 8: CV Review and Approval Process

Objective: Learn how to refine and improve your CV.

  • How to review your CV critically
  • Seeking feedback and making improvements
  • Final approval checklist
