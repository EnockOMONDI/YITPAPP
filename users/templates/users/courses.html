Youth Impact Training Programme

Your 9-Week Transformation Journey.

Course Overview

The Youth Impact Training Programme (YITP) is a 9-week virtual training designed to equip young people with essential life skills, career readiness, and entrepreneurial knowledge. Our mission is to empower youth with practical skills and a success-driven mindset to help them excel in both employment and business.

Why Choose YITP?:

    • Learn practical, real-world skills for the 21st century
    • Mentorship & networking opportunities with industry leaders
    • Connect to job, scholarship, & business opportunities
    • Earn a certification upon successful completion

Course Structure & Units:

  • Understanding Purpose in Life (UPL) (10 sessions, 2 weeks part-time)

    Discover your strengths, passion, and life goals.

  • Personal Initiative (PI) (15 sessions, 1 week part-time)

    Build confidence, self-leadership, and proactive decision-making skills.

  • The Power of Mindset (TPM) (10 sessions, 2 weeks part-time)

    Shift your perspective to a growth mindset for career and business success.

  • Soft Skills for Transitioning from School to Work (TSW-SSS) (10 sessions, 2 weeks part-time)

    Master communication, teamwork, problem-solving, and workplace professionalism.

  • Entrepreneurship & Business Alignment (ESBA) (10 sessions, 2 weeks part-time)

    Learn how to start, manage, and grow a successful business.

  • CV Writing (CW) (1-day, 2-hour session with individual follow-up)

    Create a professional CV & cover letter that stands out to employers.
