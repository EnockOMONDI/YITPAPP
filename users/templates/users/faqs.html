Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Youth Impact Global and our training programs

Youth Impact Global is a training program designed to equip young people aged 15-25 with essential skills, knowledge, and mindset for success in their careers or entrepreneurial journeys. We offer practical training, mentorship, and networking opportunities to help youth become effective leaders and change-makers.

The program runs for nine weeks (two months and one week) with virtual training sessions. After completion, participants receive one year of follow-up support including mentorship, networking, and business/career coaching.

The program currently has a special offer of KES 5,000 ($39), reduced from the regular price of KES 35,000 ($270). This can be paid in two easy installments of KES 2,500 ($20) each. This offer is limited to the first 300 enrollments.

The program is open to:
  • High School Students (15+ years)
  • College & University Students
  • Graduates & Young Professionals
  • Job Seekers & Employees

The program covers six main units:
  • Understanding Purpose in Life
  • Transition from School to World
  • Entrepreneurship Skills
  • The Power of Mindset
  • CV Writing & Professional Growth
  • Personal Initiative

Registration is simple:
  1. Fill out the enrollment form online
  2. Choose your payment plan
  3. Send payment confirmation to info@youthimpactglobal.com
  4. Receive your course schedule and start learning!
